Safety Advisories

Stay informed about the latest safety guidelines and protect yourself from various types of crimes

Banking Fraud Prevention

  • • Never share OTP with anyone
  • • Verify bank communications independently
  • • Use secure networks for transactions
  • • Report suspicious activities immediately

Cyber Security Tips

  • • Use strong, unique passwords
  • • Avoid suspicious email attachments
  • • Keep software updated
  • • Be cautious on social media

Lottery Scam Awareness

  • • No legitimate lottery requires payment
  • • Verify through official channels
  • • Don't provide personal information
  • • Report fake lottery communications
