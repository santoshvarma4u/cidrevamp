Safety Advisories & Awareness

Stay informed about latest threats and protection measures to keep yourself and your family safe

Cyber Fraud Alert

Beware of fake lottery messages, OTP frauds, and suspicious banking calls. Never share your personal information.

Banking Security

Always verify banking communications directly with your bank. Use secure networks for online transactions.

Social Media Safety

Protect your privacy settings, avoid sharing personal details, and report suspicious accounts immediately.
