Telangana Police First Duty Meet The Duty Meet is scheduled to be organized in the month of October 2024 at the Telangana Police Academy, by CID, TG, Hyderabad.


MEMO of Duty Meet

The Telangana State 1st Police Duty Meet will be held in the month of October 2024 at the Telangana Police Academy, organized by CID, Telangana, Hyderabad. The following events will take place during the meet:

  1. Scientific Aids to Investigation
  2. Anti-Sabotage Check
  3. Dog Squad Competition
  4. Computer Awareness
  5. Photography
  6. Videography

(The details with regard to syllabus and guidelines of each event are uploaded in the Telangana State Police Intranet Portal @ intranet.tspolice.gov.in for your reference and guidance)

Rewards and Medals Know more

Rewards are being given by the State Government to the winners of All India Police Duty Meet vide GO. Ms. No. 13 Home (WNT) Dept. Dt. 03.02.2014, to encourage the State Police force to improve their professional standards.
  • Gold Medal - 3 lakhs & 3 advance increments
  • Silver Medal - 2 lakhs & 2 advance increments
  • Bronze - 1 lakhs & 1 advance increments
  • Rs. 50,000/- to each member of the team securing Winners trophy
  • Rs. 30,000/- to each member of the team securing Runners-up trophy
  • Rs. 20,000/- to each member of the team securing III position

Trophy Conditions and Criteria Know more

  • Individual medals of Gold, Silver and Bronze will be awarded in each event. Apart from individual medals the team scoring the highest number of marks in each category will be awarded Winners trophy and Runners Trophy.
  • In Scientific Aids to Investigation event, Hard liner Trophy (III Best) and Individual Championship Trophy will be awarded to the candidate (SI/CI) securing highest marks in all the events.
  • Best Dog Trophy is awarded to the top-performing dog and handler team across three challenging events: Tracking, Explosive Detection, and Narcotics Detection. This prestigious honor recognizes excellence in the dog's skills and the handler’s ability to effectively guide the dog in each event.
  • Overall Team Championship Trophy will be awarded to the team which secures maximum number of medals and trophies in all the 6 categories.
